SVF System

SVF System

Streamlined Visa Processing (SVP) system is a new student visa processing system which has been available since 24 March 2012. Under SVP system a student should have his or her course offered from the SVP providers only. Student visa applications are lodged with a Confirmation of Enrolment from the SVP providers. Students can enrol in advanced diploma, bachelor, masers or doctoral programmes. SVP arrangement reduces the evidentiary requirements for a student visa regardless of the applicant’s country of origin. On this note students are assessed against the requirements outlined for financial assessment level 1 countries no matter which country the student is from.
